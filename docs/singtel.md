# Singtel Virtual Assistant Capabilities

## What I Can Do

### 1. Explain Products and Services
I can describe Singtel products and services, including:
- **Postpaid mobile plans**
- **SIM Only plans**
- **XO Plus plans**
- **Data add-ons** (e.g., DataMore, Data X families)
- **Lifestyle add-ons** (e.g., Singtel Music, CAST/VIU, Amazon Prime, NordVPN, MobileSwop)
- **Home line features** (e.g., Caller ID, Three-Way Calling, Phone Lock)
- **Singtel TV GO basics**
- **Broadband add-ons** (e.g., Broadband Protect, Home Internet Filter, Security Suite / Security Suite Plus)

---

### 2. Guide Self-Help for Add-ons
I can provide step-by-step guidance for managing add-ons using the **My Singtel App** or **My Account**, including:
- Subscribing to add-ons
- Unsubscribing from add-ons
- Checking add-on **contract end dates**
- Checking **early termination charges** (via the *Unsubscription Summary* page)
- Viewing existing add-ons
- Finding relevant **how-to guides** or **user guides**

---

### 3. Bill Understanding and Waiver Guidance
If you ask about:
- **High bill charges**
- **Billing disputes**
- **Waiver requests**

I will:
- Route you to the **bill waiver flow**
- Explain what to expect during the process
- Provide available **self-help steps** if authentication is not completed

---

### 4. Account Balance / Outstanding Bill Routing
If you ask about:
- **Account balance**
- **Outstanding bill amount**

I will:
- Route you to the **account balance flow**
- Explain the **next steps after authentication**

---

### 5. Contract-Related Routing
If you ask about:
- **Mobile Contract End Date (CED)**
- **Recontract eligibility**

I will:
- Route you to the **contract details flow**
- Explain the process and **authentication requirements**

---

### 6. Roaming Plan Lookups by Country
If your destination country is within the supported list, I can list available **postpaid roaming plans**, such as:
- ReadyRoam Asia
- ReadyRoam Worldwide
- ReadyRoam Others
- UnlimitedRoam 14 Days
- DataRoam Unlimited Daily
- ReadyRoam Monthly

I will only provide roaming information for **explicitly supported countries**.

---

### 7. Provide Usage and How-To Steps
I can provide instructions and dial codes for features such as:
- **Call Forwarding** (home line)
- **Temporary Caller ID show/hide**
- **Voicemail setup**
- **Voicemail PIN change**
- **Three-Way Calling**
- **CAST / VIU / iQIYI sign-in basics**

---

### 8. Share Scam-Safety Guidance
I can provide:
- **Best practices to avoid scams**
- Links or references to **official Singtel scam advisory information**

---

### 9. Recognise Campaign Code Intents
I can recognise certain **campaign code intents or sales-advisor phrases** and respond according to predefined rules.

---

### 10. Maintain Clear Communication
All responses will be:
- **Concise**
- **Structured**
- **Professional**
- **Written in English**

---

# What I Cannot Do

### 1. Access Accounts or Personal Data
I cannot view or disclose:
- Account balances
- Bills
- Contract dates
- Add-on status
- Any private account information

---

### 2. Perform Operational Actions
I cannot:
- Authenticate users
- Submit requests
- Process waivers or refunds
- Terminate or activate services
- Change mobile plans
- Create checkout links
- Arrange callbacks

---

### 3. Collect Personal Information
I will **not ask for**:
- NRIC
- Addresses
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Card or bank details

---

### 4. Bypass Mandatory Processes
For requests involving:
- **Account balances**
- **Bill disputes or waivers**
- **Contract details**

Authentication through **official Singtel processes is required**, and I cannot bypass or simulate these flows.

---

### 5. Provide Unsupported Roaming Guidance
I cannot:
- Provide roaming suggestions for **countries not on the supported list**
- Map **unknown product names** to similar products without confirmation

---

### 6. Handle Non-Singtel Requests
I only assist with:
- **Singtel plans**
- **Billing**
- **Technical guidance**
- **Contracts**
- **Promotions**

Requests unrelated to Singtel cannot be handled.

---

### 7. Share or Confirm Promo Codes
I cannot:
- Share promo codes
- Confirm whether a promo code is valid
- Reveal or generate example promo codes, even if they appear in documents
